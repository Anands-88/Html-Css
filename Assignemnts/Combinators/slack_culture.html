Working and Thriving

We want people to thrive at Slack; we believe you do your best work when you feel your best. Here are a just a few benefits we're proud to provide our
team with.

Learning & development

Receive an annual allowance for professional development, plus a monthly bonus you can use towards anything. That means anything: a gym membership, your pet sitter, a new work look. Whatever matters most to you.

Health & wellness

Free medical, dental care and eye care for you and your family - Slack pays 100% of your premiums.

Parental leave

A lot of us have families, of all sorts of shapes and sizes, we welcome each new addition with generous parental leave and "bonding leave".

Happier weekdays

A culture light on meetingss (or that's our aim, at lease) with guest speakers and opportunities to get to kno your team better through events like weekly catered lunches and breakfasts, Thursday Social and game nights. But we don't stays in the office 24/7.
